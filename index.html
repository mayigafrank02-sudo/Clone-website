Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Winner Saving Group work?

Each member saves UGX 2,500 daily. Each week, one member receives the entire weekly pool. Rotation order is predetermined and visible to all members. Fines of UGX 500 are automatically applied for missed payments.

How do I make my daily payment?

Payments are made directly to the group's mobile money account. The admin will provide payment details. Payments should be made by 10 PM daily to avoid fines.

What happens if I miss a payment?

If you miss a daily payment, an automatic fine of UGX 500 is added to your account. You must pay both the missed savings and fine before your rotation week.

How can I join the group?

Click "Request to Join" on the login page. The admin will review your request and approve if there's space. Once approved, you'll receive login details.

How is the rotation order determined?

The rotation order is initially set by the admin based on joining date or other fair criteria. It can be adjusted by the admin if needed, with notification to all members.

How do I access my account?

Use your phone number and the last 5 digits of your phone number as password. For example, if your phone is 0755123456, your password is 23456.
